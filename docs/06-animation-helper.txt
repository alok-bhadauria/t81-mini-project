06-ANIMATION-HELPER

---------------------------------------------------------------------

01. Overview

This document serves as the master blueprint for the 3D Animation mapping pipeline.
Animators must create Blender animations (.glb) and name them exactly according to the IDs listed below.
The backend pipeline (specifically 02_animation_service.py) relies natively on checking the external flat JSON indices (app/data/gesture_map.json and sentiment_map.json) to trigger the Three.js avatar accurately avoiding tight coupling.

---------------------------------------------------------------------

02. Sentiment Animations (saXXX)
These animations apply natively to the face and torso to convey the emotional tone of the sentence.
They loop underneath the hand gestures.

ID      | Emotion Trigger    | Description
sa001   | HAPPY              | Smiling, relaxed shoulders, open body language
sa002   | SAD                | Frowning, slumped shoulders, downcast eyes
sa003   | NEUTRAL            | Rest pose, neutral expression, subtle idle breathing
sa004   | ANGRY              | Furrowed brows, tense shoulders, sharp movements
sa005   | SURPRISED          | Raised eyebrows, wide eyes, recoil
sa006   | FEAR               | Tense posture, widened eyes, guarded hands
sa007   | DISGUST            | Wrinkled nose, retracted head, scowling
sa008   | EXCITED            | Wide smile, rapid eye movement, energetic
sa009   | BORED              | Drooping eyelids, shifting weight, sighing posture
sa010   | CONFUSED           | Squinting, head tilt, asymmetrical brow
